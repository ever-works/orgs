Customer Operations is where GreenRoute wins or loses each individual customer. Reyes runs the team and owns the record and the calendar: quote requests are worked the moment they arrive, estimates go out inside their SLA, and confirmed jobs land in geography-first clusters that keep crews on route-dense days. Wren owns the voice — every confirmation, reminder, follow-up, and complaint response comes from one author with one tone, triggered by Reyes's timing rules. The team's standing promise: no quote request ages past its SLA, no customer message goes out in a second voice, and no record sits in two lifecycle stages at once.
