The Growth team runs GreenRoute's demand engine as one loop rather than three silos. Finch reads the outside world — competitors, seasonality, demand signals — and delivers at most three findings a week, each tied to a decision. Tally reads the inside world — bookings, route economics, estimation accuracy — and maintains the seasonal forecast that tells the team when demand will move before it moves. Sloane manages the team and acts on both: local-search work and campaigns timed ahead of the demand curve, measured all the way to booked jobs, never stopping at clicks. The combined weekly rollup goes to Harper as one picture: what the market did, what the numbers say, and what changes next week.
