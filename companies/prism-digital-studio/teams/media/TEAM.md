The Media team is the studio's visual unit, led by the VFX Artist. Every piece of visual work — effects sequences, shader-driven graphics, animated loops and stickers — follows the same discipline: storyboard and shot plan first, direction check on anything complex, then production to the exact spec each shot entry defines. The team hands finished renders to the Producer's QA gate like every other deliverable, and feeds embedded motion work into application builds through Studio Lead-sequenced Tasks, so visual craft plugs into the wider pipeline instead of running beside it.
