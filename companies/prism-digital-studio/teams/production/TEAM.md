The Production team owns both ends of the studio pipeline: the Producer shapes raw client requests into briefs at intake, and every finished deliverable passes back through the Producer's delivery-checklist QA before the Studio Lead sees it. In between, the App Builder ships applications and the Document Producer ships professional documents, each working strictly from a written brief and handing back explicit artifacts — builds with build notes, documents with delivery notes. The team's contract with the studio is symmetry: nothing enters production unshaped, and nothing leaves it unchecked.
