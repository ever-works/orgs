Partnering is where the standing population turns when something comes up — a leave, a hard conversation, a role change, a "can I do this," a conflict that needs a neutral hand. The People Partner answers consistently from policy, coaches managers through the situations they own, handles sensitive matters with discretion and a clear record, and knows exactly which edges to escalate. Alongside, the Employee Experience Lead works the population-level view: owning the self-serve FAQ, measuring where the lifecycle predictably goes badly, and clustering the noise of individual questions into a short list of root causes worth fixing. The team's standing rule is that one person's question is usually the population's problem — so every answer is consistent and sourced, and every recurring friction becomes an owned fix routed to whoever owns that surface, not a complaint that gets answered fifty times.
