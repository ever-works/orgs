The strategy team owns everything between "the audience cares about this" and "this script is ready to produce". Rex scouts the niche and delivers evidence-backed topic briefs; Iris builds them into a calendar where every slot is a written bet and no two adjacent slots repeat a hook pattern; Sable turns commissioned slots into scripts whose openings are drafted hardest and tested first. The team's gate is its identity: Iris alone promotes scripts to production, always against the explicit hook criteria, and a hole in the calendar is always preferred to a weak opening. Each cycle the team rebuilds its slate on Quill's pattern findings, so the calendar is never a repeat of last cycle's habits — it is a response to what the audience most recently rewarded.
