The growth team closes the studio's loop. Vesper runs the publishing calendar — concrete slots at a committed cadence, packaging that sharpens each hook's promise without replacing it, and a clean metadata ledger recorded at publish time. Quill turns that ledger plus performance data into the cycle's pattern report: which hook shapes hold viewers past the opening, which formats are decaying, and where the quality gate needs recalibration. The team's discipline is separation of observation from action — Quill reports at the pattern level with sample sizes stated, Vesper flags anomalies early rather than diagnosing them, and both feed their findings upward so Marlowe and Iris make the strategy calls with evidence in hand. Cadence kept, labels clean, findings honest: that is the whole job.
