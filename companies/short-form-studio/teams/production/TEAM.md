The production team owns everything between "this script passed the gate" and "this video is approved for publishing". Otis plans each assembly — visual treatment, narration spec, and the variation choices that keep this video distinct from the last several — then sequences Cadence's voice work ahead of Wren's cut so picture always serves narration. The team runs on two hard rules: the realized opening must deliver exactly what the gated hook promised, and no two consecutive videos may share the same combination of visual rhythm and voice treatment. Every cut passes Otis's three-question review before leaving the team, revisions happen in one consolidated round, and anything that cannot be realized to standard is escalated to Marlowe out loud rather than shipped quietly compromised.
