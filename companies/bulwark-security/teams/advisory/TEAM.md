# Advisory

The advisory team is deliberately separate from the people who found the bugs:
its job is to challenge, not to cheer. Every finding that reaches a client
passes through here, where the report writer tests the evidence against the
claim, holds severity to the same standard across application and
infrastructure lanes, and rewrites everything for the two readers who matter —
the executive deciding what to fund and the engineer deciding what to change
first. The team's output is the firm's actual product: a report that tells the
truth at a glance, separates verified claims from open questions, and never
inflates a rating to make an engagement look busier than it was.
