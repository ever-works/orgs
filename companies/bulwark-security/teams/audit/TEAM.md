# Audit

The audit team does the firm's hands-on assessment work, and it does all of it
inside a signed scope. The pentest planner turns each accepted engagement into
that scope; the threat modeler maps where the risk concentrates so hours are
spent deliberately; the appsec auditor leads execution, keeping the
application lane and handing infrastructure surfaces to the infra auditor.
The team's hard rule is verification: nothing leaves this team as a finding
unless it was reproduced within the rules of engagement and carries its
evidence with it — suspicions travel separately, labeled as open questions.
Anything that would cross from assessment into attack stops here and goes up
to the CISO.
