The Writing team owns the episode from creative brief to script lock. The Head Writer builds the five-act outline and drafts the script, then runs the internal polish loop: the Dialogue Writer makes every line on-voice and speakable, the Script Doctor diagnoses structural failures while they are still cheap, and the Pacing Analyst holds the draft to the timing budget and the show's own retention history. The Head Writer merges all of it into one voice and submits through the Creative Director to Narrative QA. Everything downstream — boards, footage, cut, mix — is built on this team's output, which is why its internal loop is deliberately harsher than the gate it feeds.
