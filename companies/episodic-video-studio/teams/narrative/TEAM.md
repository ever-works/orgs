The Narrative team is the custodian of everything the series has promised its audience. The Story Lead owns canon and rules on it in writing; the Character Director maintains the profiles and arc maps that keep every character recognizable and every change earned; the Lore Researcher keeps the canon log current, grounds the fiction in researched fact, and hunts contradictions before viewers do. The team annotates every episode brief before writing starts and reviews every submission draft before it faces the gate — it is consulted early precisely so it rarely has to say no late.
