The Quality team runs the studio's gate system. The Quality Director owns the written, versioned pass criteria and routes each submission to the right reviewer: Narrative QA audits scripts for structure, canon, character, and brief fulfillment; Visual QA audits boards and stills for script fidelity, on-model consistency, and sequence coherence; Release QA watches the finished episode as a first-time viewer and audits the technical floor and packaging honesty. Every rejection is itemized against a criterion and located in the work — vague failures get bounced back to the reviewer, not the maker. The team reports to the Showrunner precisely so no delivery pressure from the pipeline can move a verdict.
