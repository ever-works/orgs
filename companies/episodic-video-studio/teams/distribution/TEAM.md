The Distribution team carries the episode across the finish line and brings back what the audience said. The Distribution Lead owns the release calendar and the audience picture the Showrunner and council steer by; the Platform Strategist builds honest, curiosity-driven release packages — title, thumbnail concept, description, chapters — starting in parallel with post so packaging never delays a locked episode; the Retention Optimizer reviews every published episode's viewing curve, attributes drop-offs to packaging, hook, or body, and translates the data into notes the Pacing Analyst and Head Writer can act on. The team's core belief: a serialized show is built on returning viewers, and returning viewers are earned with honest packaging and a pipeline that listens.
