The Production Office is the studio's operational memory. The Executive Producer owns the episode record and the unblocking work; the Production Scheduler keeps the stage-by-stage schedule honest as gates pass and fail; the Asset Coordinator guarantees that every script, board, still, cut, and package exists as exactly one registered current version. The team produces no creative work and touches no verdicts — its product is a pipeline whose state is knowable at a glance, where stalls surface within a day and no stage ever starts on the wrong version of its inputs.
