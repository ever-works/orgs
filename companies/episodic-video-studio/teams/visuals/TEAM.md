The Visuals team owns the episode from script lock to visual lock. The Visual Director holds the show's visual language and reviews every stage; the Cinematography Designer decides what each shot is for and how framing, lens, and light argue it; the Storyboard Artist proves the scenes cut together as annotated panels; the Visual Prompt Engineer converts approved boards into versioned, reproducible prompt sets with a descriptor bank that keeps recurring characters and places on-model across the season. The team's discipline is strict ordering — script before shots, shots before boards, boards before prompts — because every unlocked upstream step multiplies waste downstream.
