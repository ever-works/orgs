Post-Production owns the episode from visual lock to picture lock. The Post-Production Lead sequences the work — rough cut, continuity review, fine cut, mix, full internal review — and arbitrates when picture, sound, and continuity pull in different directions. The Editor assembles and refines the cut against boards and act timings; the Audio Engineer realizes the cue plan, narration, and ambience into a mix where every line is intelligible; the Continuity Supervisor audits the cut against canon, the boards' continuity annotations, and every previous episode. The team's law is the change log: deviations from the locked plan happen, but each one is written down with its reason before Release QA reads it.
