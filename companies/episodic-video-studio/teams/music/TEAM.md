The Music team is the Music Director's domain: the series music guide, the recurring themes that track character arcs, and the per-episode cue plan and sound design brief that tell Post-Production exactly what every musical moment must do to the viewer — including the moments that must stay silent. The Music Director reads the brief when the writers do and the locked script the day it locks, so the score is designed alongside the story, and reviews every realized cue against the brief before the mix goes to a gate.
