# Aurora Creative Agency

A prebuilt AI brand-and-content agency for Ever Works. Aurora turns raw business goals into positioning, campaign briefs, copy, content calendars, and motion assets — with a strict "no brief, no production; no delivery without review" pipeline.

## Org structure

**Creative Director (Marlow Hale)** — root; routes work, approves briefs, final sign-off.

- **Strategy team** — manager: Brand Strategist (Ines Varga)
  - Copywriter (Priya Sethi)
  - Social Media Manager (Dara Okafor)
- **Production team** — manager: Art Director (Theo Marchetti)
  - Motion Designer (Kenji Abara)

6 agents, 2 teams, 3 skills: `brand-voice-guide`, `campaign-brief`, `content-calendar`.

## What to expect after import

Send a Task to the company (a campaign request, a rebrand question, "plan our launch content") and the Creative Director will classify it, route strategy questions to the Brand Strategist, and fan out production only once a signed brief exists. Deliverables come back as explicit artifacts — briefs, voice guides, copy decks, calendars, and asset delivery notes — each reviewed twice (team lead, then Creative Director) before reaching you.
