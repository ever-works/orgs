The Strategy team owns everything upstream of production: discovery, brand voice, campaign briefs, copy, and the content calendar. The Brand Strategist runs the room — writing the briefs the whole agency executes against and first-reviewing everything the Copywriter and Social Media Manager produce before it travels up to the Creative Director. The team's output is the source of truth the Production team builds from; when Strategy is sloppy, every downstream asset inherits the sloppiness, so this team's quality bar is the agency's quality bar.
