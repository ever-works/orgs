The Production team executes what Strategy has decided. The Art Director translates each signed campaign brief into written visual direction and a checkable asset list, then hands production Tasks to the Motion Designer, whose drafts return for first review before anything reaches the Creative Director. The team's contract with the rest of the agency is precision: every asset ships in the exact format, dimension, and naming the asset list specifies, and every creative choice traces back to a line in the brief or the direction — never to an undocumented preference.
