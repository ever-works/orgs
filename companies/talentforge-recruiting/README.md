# Talentforge Recruiting

Talentforge is a prebuilt AI-company package for the Ever Works orgs catalog. It models a
recruiting and talent-operations firm that runs the full hiring pipeline — from intake and
sourcing through structured interviewing, offer negotiation, and pipeline analysis — as a
team of coordinated agents. Import it when you want a hiring function that turns an open role
into an evaluated shortlist and, eventually, a signed offer, using an evidence-based,
candidate-first process rather than gut-feel hiring.

## How it works

Every role enters through the **Head of Talent**, who runs intake: the vague ask becomes a
written spec with named competencies, a scorecard the whole loop grades against, a target
market, and a hiring bar. That spec becomes the role's Work, decomposed into Tasks — a
sourcing Task, an interview-design Task, a coordination Task — each with an owner and a
definition of done. Candidates advance only on graded evidence against the scorecard, and
each handoff is logged on the candidate's Task so the trail from first-touch to offer stays
intact.

## Org structure

**Head of Talent** (Maya Okonkwo) is the single root. Reporting to her: the Sourcer,
Recruiter, Interview Designer, Employer Brand Lead, Offer Negotiator, and Pipeline Analyst.
The Coordinator reports to the Recruiter.

### Teams

- **Sourcing** — manager: Sourcer (Diego Ramos); includes the Employer Brand Lead (Nadia Farouk).
  Fills the top of the funnel: builds candidate-sourcing plans, runs personalized outreach,
  and shapes the employer story so strong candidates say yes to a first conversation.
- **Pipeline** — manager: Recruiter (Hannah Bergström); includes the Coordinator (Leah Kim)
  and the Interview Designer (Sam Adeyemi). Runs candidates from first screen to final
  decision: structured interview design, fast scheduling, and evidence-based evaluation.

### Skills

- **candidate-sourcing-plan** — turn a role's intake into a targeted sourcing plan.
- **structured-interview-kit** — build a bias-resistant, competency-mapped interview loop.
- **candidate-packet** — assemble interview assignments and debrief evidence in one place.
- **offer-strategy** — set target, band, levers, and walk line before extending an offer.
- **pipeline-health-report** — measure the funnel and turn bottlenecks into owned fixes.

## What to expect after import

You'll get one company with 8 agents, 2 teams, and 5 skills, wired so the reporting lines,
team memberships, and skill references all resolve. Agents receive Tasks, work within the
role's Work, use their attached skills, and coordinate handoffs through Task updates. Point
the Head of Talent at an open role to start an intake, or hand the Sourcer a role spec to
begin filling the funnel. There are no external dependencies or credits — every file in this
package is original.
