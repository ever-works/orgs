Sourcing owns everything that happens before a candidate enters the pipeline. Working from the Head of Talent's intake spec, the Sourcer builds a candidate-sourcing plan, identifies the pools where the target profile actually lives, and runs personalized outreach that leads with fit rather than volume — qualifying for genuine interest before passing anyone on. The Employer Brand Lead makes that outreach land, sharpening job descriptions and the outreach narrative, owning the candidate experience as a brand surface, and turning the objections the Sourcer hears in the field into message and process fixes. The team's standing rule is honesty at the top of the funnel: sell the truth of a role well, never a fiction, because a candidate drawn in by a mis-set expectation is a decline at offer time or a resignation within the year.
