The Revenue team is the human layer on top of a product-led motion: it exists for the accounts where a person genuinely unlocks value, not to replace self-serve. Darius (Sales Lead) runs discipline-first discovery on product-qualified accounts handed up by Growth — naming the buyer's real problem before any demo, qualifying hard, and disqualifying fast and kindly when a human isn't needed. Won accounts pass cleanly to Lena (Customer Success Lead) with their discovery context intact, so onboarding starts from what the buyer actually wanted; from there she protects time-to-first-win, watches account health for early churn signals, and finds expansion by surfacing the next real problem worth solving. The team's throughline: every objection, stall, and churn reason is captured as structured signal and routed back to Product and Growth, so revenue feeds the loop instead of running beside it.
