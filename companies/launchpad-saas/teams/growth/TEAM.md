The Growth team treats acquisition, activation, retention, and referral as one compounding loop rather than a set of disconnected campaigns. Sofia (Growth Lead) keeps a funnel scorecard, finds the stage that leaks worst, and runs a single honest experiment against it before moving on. Jonah (Content Marketer) owns the top of the loop — problem-first content that brings the right people to the product and primes them to sign up — while Mina (Lifecycle Marketer) owns the in-product stages, moving new users from signup to first win to habit with behavior-triggered sequences. The team's rule: every stage has a rate, every experiment targets one stage and one number, and when the loop is blocked by the product itself, the team packages the evidence and hands it to Product as a bet rather than working around it.
