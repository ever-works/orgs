The Product team owns everything between "Priya funded this bet" and "this shipped, it's measurable, and we know whether it worked." Theo (Head of Product) decomposes each mandate into scoped Tasks, sequences discovery and PRD ahead of design and build, and is the single review gate — nothing is done until he accepts it against its definition of done. Amara (Product Manager) writes the PRD that turns a fuzzy bet into a testable slice with a number attached, and Noah (UX Designer) designs the flow with the first-win activation path built in rather than bolted on. The team's standing promise: every feature ships as the thinnest slice that answers the bet, instrumented so the loop can learn from it, and activation is treated as a product requirement — not something Growth is left to fix afterward.
