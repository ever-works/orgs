Growth keeps learners and clients flowing into the academy. Callum picks the segments, ranks the targets, and qualifies every warm reply into a one-page brief for Noor to close; Zadie writes the short, honestly personalized openers and — when a prospect bites — delivers a real five-message sample lesson so the product pitches itself. The team's rules of engagement: no send without a researched personal detail, three touches maximum per prospect, warm replies routed same-day, and a weekly funnel report that always ends in a decision.
