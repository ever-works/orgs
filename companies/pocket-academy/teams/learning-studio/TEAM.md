The Learning Studio owns everything a learner experiences: Tobin compresses each topic into a five-message lesson that survives a three-second glance, Isla proves whether it landed with a three-question quiz and keeps it landed through spaced reviews at 1, 3, and 7 days, and Maren holds the format line — approving every lesson before first delivery, ordering client curricula, and rewriting whatever the quiz data condemns. The studio's standing promises: one concept per lesson, no lesson ships unreviewed, every completed lesson enters the review ladder, and a topic counts as learned only after three consecutive passes.
