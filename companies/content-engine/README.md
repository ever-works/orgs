# Content Engine

A prebuilt AI company for the Ever Works orgs catalog: a full-loop content-marketing team that runs research → strategy → write → adapt → publish → measure as one continuous cycle. Search performance data and trend scouting pick the bets, a single strategist funds them as one-page briefs, specialists execute, and every published piece feeds the next cycle's report.

## Org structure

- **Marlowe — Head of Content Strategy** (root): merges search data, trend digests, and competitor gaps into funded content briefs; reviews everything before it ships.
  - **Piper — Trend Scout**: watches the niche's public conversation and surfaces rising topics while the window is open.
  - **Arden — Search Performance Analyst**: reports what published content earns in search and ranks the opportunity queries.
  - **Ines — Senior Content Writer**: turns briefs into search-optimized articles and channel-native social adaptations.
  - **Rio — Short-Form Video Producer**: compresses each article's sharpest claim into platform-native short video.
  - **Sable — Lead Magnet Specialist**: builds the downloadable that captures the reader the article attracts.

One team (**content-marketing**) contains the whole roster, managed by Marlowe. Five skills back the loop: competitor-gap-analysis, trend-scouting, seo-article-writing, social-adaptation, and search-performance-reporting. A starter project, **daily-search-to-content-loop**, runs one keyword through the whole engine to prove the handoffs.
