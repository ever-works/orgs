The content marketing team owns everything between "what should we say next?" and "here is what saying it earned us." Marlowe funds a small number of content bets per cycle, each anchored to a demand signal supplied by Arden's search data or Piper's trend digest, and each executed through a brief with one owner and numeric success criteria. Ines writes the anchor article, Rio and Sable spin it into video and capture assets, and every deliverable passes back through Marlowe before it ships. The team's standing promise: no piece is published without a signal behind it, and no published piece escapes its post-publication reading in the next report.
