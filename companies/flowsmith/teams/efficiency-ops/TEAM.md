Efficiency-ops never touches a product stream; it audits the machine that runs them. On a standing cadence — and whenever the pipeline throws a cost or permission signal — Mara measures session health, Theo trims the access surface to demonstrated need, and Nadia traces where tokens and time actually go. The output is always the same shape: a ranked recommendation list where every item carries an expected saving and an adoption cost, delivered to Ansel, who alone decides which recommendations become pipeline rules. The team's discipline is follow-through: every adopted recommendation is re-measured, and predictions that missed get recorded like any other correction.
