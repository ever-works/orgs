The engineering team owns everything between "Ansel accepted this request" and "Petra certified this change". Work moves through it in one direction — Ivo grounds the research, Wren turns findings into an executable plan, implementation happens step by step against that plan, Caleb runs down anything that breaks, and Petra re-derives every claim before the Review gate closes. The team's standing promises: no plan without research behind it, no certification by whoever implemented, and no closed gate without its lessons written to the ledger.
