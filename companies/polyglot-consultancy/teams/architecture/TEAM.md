The architecture team is the CTO's cross-cutting arm: the solutions architect drafts option papers, interface contracts, and migration plans for load-bearing decisions, while the security reviewer reads designs and changes for how they can be abused. Neither owns an engagement; both touch every one, and their sign-off gates anything that changes system shape or attack surface.
