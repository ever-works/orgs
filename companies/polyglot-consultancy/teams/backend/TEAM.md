The backend practice builds services, APIs, and server-side logic in whichever language the client's stack demands, with one specialist owning each language family. The lead cuts workstreams into slices, guards the API surface, and reviews every change before it leaves the practice; slices ship with tests, migration stories, and written handoffs.
