# Polyglot Consultancy

A full-service, many-languages software consultancy: backend services in Go, Python, and Node, frontend and mobile apps, cloud platform and CI/CD, data and ML delivery, and a dedicated quality practice — with architecture and security review wired into every engagement rather than bolted on at the end.

## How it is organized

- **ceo** — scopes engagements into briefs; the only root
  - **cto** — decomposes briefs, routes workstreams, owns technical standards
    - **Backend practice** — backend-lead, go-engineer, python-engineer, node-engineer
    - **Frontend & Mobile practice** — frontend-mobile-lead, react-engineer, vue-engineer, mobile-engineer
    - **Platform & DevOps practice** — platform-devops-lead, cloud-engineer, ci-engineer, sre-engineer
    - **Data practice** — data-lead, analytics-engineer, database-engineer, ml-engineer
    - **Quality practice** — quality-lead, test-engineer, performance-engineer
    - **Architecture team** (managed by the cto) — solutions-architect, security-reviewer

23 agents, 6 teams, 30 skills. Skills are practical playbooks: language service playbooks (Go/Python/Node, React/Vue, mobile), api-design, code-review, migration-plan, perf-audit, security-audit, observability, release-management, and more.

## Curation note

This package is curated from an upstream org of 49 agents and 66 skills. We kept the load-bearing shape — an executive pair, discipline leads, and deep specialists per practice — and trimmed niche platform roles (CMS, commerce plugins, game and embedded work) plus per-framework duplicates in favor of one strong specialist per language or platform family.
