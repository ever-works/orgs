# Engineering

The engineering team turns the CTO's slice plans into working software. Backend and
frontend build against contracts agreed before either starts, so integration is a
meeting of two finished halves rather than a negotiation between two guesses; DevOps
keeps the pipeline, environments, and observability that let those halves reach
production the same way every time. The team's definition of done is deliberately
strict — implemented, tested at the owned seam, documented for deploy, and handed to
the quality team with an honest note about what to probe first.
