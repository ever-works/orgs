The product team owns everything between "Nova funded this goal" and "this works and is ready to launch". Harper decomposes mandates into tasks with explicit definitions of done, sequences research ahead of building so Sage's findings shape what Kai implements rather than second-guess it afterward, and reviews every deliverable before it counts as finished. The team's standing promise to the rest of the company: nothing leaves this team unverified, and nothing enters it without a named outcome.
