The growth team owns every touchpoint between the company and the outside world. Vega keeps a single positioning source of truth and routes work from it: Lyric writes the assets, Dash works the pipeline, Juno answers the customers. The team runs a deliberate feedback loop — Juno's tagged customer themes and Dash's objection log flow back through Vega into better copy, sharper positioning, and product signals for Harper — so every customer conversation makes the next one easier.
