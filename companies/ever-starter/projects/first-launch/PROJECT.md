# First Launch

Take the company from a funded idea to a credible public debut: a scoped MVP definition, a clear-eyed view of the competitive field, and landing-page copy ready to publish. This project is deliberately small — its job is to produce the three artifacts every launch depends on, prove the company's decompose–execute–review loop end to end, and surface anything about the idea that should change before serious build effort starts.

Harper (pm) owns the project and sequences it so learning precedes commitment: competitor research and MVP planning run first and inform each other, and landing copy is drafted from what they conclude. The project is done when all three tasks are accepted by their reviewers and Nova has what's needed to make the launch go/no-go call.
