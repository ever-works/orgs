Support owns everything a customer touches when something goes wrong, and works to make sure they need to touch it less over time. The Support Lead keeps the queue disciplined — triaging on account risk and impact rather than arrival order, maintaining the macro library so answers stay consistent, and invoking the escalation runbook the moment a ticket outgrows the queue. The Support Agent resolves tickets fast and completely, diagnosing before answering, personalizing every macro, and confirming the fix from the customer's side before closing. The Knowledge Base Lead turns the queue's recurring patterns into help articles that prevent the next ticket, keeping the help center accurate and findable so self-serve absorbs the volume the queue would otherwise carry. The team's standing rule is that closed is not the same as fixed, and that the second time an issue appears it gets flagged, so by the third it is already an article — deflection, not heroics, is how the queue stays fast.
