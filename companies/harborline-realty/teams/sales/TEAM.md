Sales is where Harborline earns and keeps clients. Sabine runs the team and the seller side: she prices every listing from comparable evidence, drives the pre-market prep punch-list, launches the property once and correctly, and represents sellers through offers and negotiation. Elias runs the buyer side — briefing every buyer before the first tour, verifying financing, shortlisting to the stated need, and writing offers that win on strategy as well as price. Noor keeps both relationships warm, translating each transaction event into plain language and reaching out before a client has to wonder. The team's standing promise: no listing goes live under-prepared or over-priced, no offer is written without a brief behind it, and no client is left waiting in silence.
