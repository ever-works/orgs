Operations is the machine that turns a signed listing into a marketed property and a marketed property into a closed deal. Priya runs the team and owns the transaction: the moment a contract is executed she builds the deadline calendar, tracks every contingency to resolution, and drives the file to a clean closing with no missed date. Juno builds and runs each property's campaign — the right buyer, the right channels, the right sequence — and directs Rhys, who schedules and quality-checks the photography, floor plans, and video every campaign depends on, timed to land before launch. The team's standing promise: every listing launches at full strength with media that represents it honestly, and every accepted offer reaches closing on schedule with a complete, verified file.
