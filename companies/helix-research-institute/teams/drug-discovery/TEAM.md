The Drug Discovery team runs the funnel from target to prioritized lead candidates. The Drug Discovery Lead confirms the target is druggable, sets the screening strategy and decision gates, and integrates results into a ranked shortlist. The Compound Screening Analyst filters libraries down to a defensible hit list, the Molecular Docking Specialist predicts and stress-tests binding modes, and the Cheminformatics Scientist profiles properties, SAR, and ADMET liabilities. The team treats every score as a hypothesis needing independent rationale, kills weak series early, and carries developability liabilities forward as first-class data rather than footnotes.
