The Materials Science team turns candidate structures into predicted, error-bounded properties. The Materials Science Lead matches method fidelity to the decision and integrates a ranked shortlist. The Computational Materials Scientist analyzes structures and phase behavior, the Molecular Simulation Scientist runs molecular dynamics and quantum-chemistry calculations at a defensible level of theory, and the Materials Informatics Analyst trains property models that flag their own applicability domain. The team screens cheap and confirms expensive, attaches an error estimate and a method to every prediction, and validates against known references before trusting a novel result.
