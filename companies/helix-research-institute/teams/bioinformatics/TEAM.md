The Bioinformatics team owns everything from raw sequencing reads to interpretable biological signal. The Bioinformatics Lead sets the statistical design and decomposes each request into alignment-and-variant work, single-cell analysis, or evolutionary inference, then quality-checks and integrates what comes back. The Genomics Analyst calls variants and quantifies expression differences, the Single-Cell Scientist resolves cell states and populations, and the Phylogenetics Researcher reconstructs evolutionary relationships. The team's output is never a bare number — it is a QC'd result with effect sizes, uncertainty, and a record of what was filtered and why, ready to feed drug, clinical, or write-up work.
