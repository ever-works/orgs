The Scientific Communication team is the last stop before anything reaches an audience, and it runs a critique gate in the middle. The Communication Lead sequences the work so the Research Editor attacks the evidence package first — for selection bias, overclaimed causality, and non-reproducible steps — and nothing is drafted until objections are resolved or logged as caveats. Only then does the Manuscript Writer synthesize the approved package into claims-traceable prose and the Figure Designer build publication-quality visuals that carry the argument. The team's product is a reviewed, honest, decision-ready report with its limitations stated plainly and every claim traced to evidence.
