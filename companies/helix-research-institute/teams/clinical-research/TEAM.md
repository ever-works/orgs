The Clinical Research team produces evidence-graded clinical findings and defends the rigor behind them. The Clinical Research Lead frames endpoints, power, and bias controls, and integrates results with explicit evidence grades. The Clinical Data Scientist models patient outcomes and survival with reproducible pipelines, the Precision Medicine Analyst classifies variants and pharmacogenomic implications against curated evidence, and the Trial Design Reviewer stress-tests study designs before they are trusted. The team names confounders every time, grades evidence explicitly, handles patient data as sensitive by default, and never crosses from informing a decision into prescribing one.
