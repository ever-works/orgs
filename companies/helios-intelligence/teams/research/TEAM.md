The Research team owns meaning. The Research Analyst maintains the graded source map, runs focused investigations, and supplies the causal story behind what the monitors observe; the Market Watcher reads every monitor firing, verifies it against independent evidence, and decides whether it clears the bar for escalation or belongs in a scheduled snapshot. Between them, nothing reaches the Intelligence Director as "signal" until someone on this team has checked that it is real, significant, and new — and nothing ships as analysis without a graded source trail behind it.
