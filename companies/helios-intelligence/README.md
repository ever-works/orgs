# Helios Intelligence

An always-on intelligence company: continuous research, engineered monitors, and market watchlists compressed into daily briefs, verified alerts, and decision-ready digests. The operating conviction is that information is cheap and attention is not — every signal is verified before it interrupts anyone, every claim carries a paper trail, and the company audits its own hit rate weekly to keep improving its signal-to-noise.

## Org structure

- **Rhea Solano — Intelligence Director** (root): front door for all Tasks; decomposes work, sets the interruption bar, writes the daily brief and weekly review, owns the final call on what interrupts a human.
  - **Ivo Marchetti — Research Analyst** (manages the Research team): graded source map, focused research briefs, recurring digests, causal explanations behind observed signals.
  - **Tamsin Okafor — Signals Engineer** (manages the Engineering team): turns watching requests into durable monitors with written specs, backtested thresholds, and deduplicated alert plumbing.
  - **Callum Reyes — Market Watcher** (Research team): reads every monitor firing, verifies before escalating, writes market snapshots and monitoring digests.
  - **Priya Anand — Operations Assistant** (Engineering team): operational rollups, shared-note hygiene, goal register, cadence calendars.

**Teams:** Research (Research Analyst + Market Watcher) owns interpretation and verification; Engineering (Signals Engineer + Operations Assistant) owns the monitoring machinery and operational legibility.

**Skills (11):** daily-briefing, weekly-review, source-triage, research-brief, digest-writing, monitor-setup, incident-alerting, backtest-note, market-snapshot, memory-hygiene, goal-tracking.

## Curation note

The upstream concept is a hub-and-spoke autonomous intelligence organization with 4 agents and 32 scheduled skills spanning research digests, engineering pipelines, and on-chain monitoring. This adaptation curates that into a 5-agent, 11-skill company: the domain-specific pipeline skills are generalized into reusable playbooks (source grading, monitor construction, alert design, backtesting, snapshot briefing), the crypto-specific monitoring role is generalized into a market/metrics Watcher, and an explicit verification step is added between monitors firing and humans being interrupted — reflecting the original project's more recent evolution toward autonomous research and signal verification.
