The Publications team owns the last mile: from approved evidence package to shipped report. The Science Writer synthesizes the evidence map, analysis brief, and critique log into a report whose every sentence traces to a source, with confidence levels carried in the text and limitations written to be read. The Lab Coordinator then runs the release pass — citation trace, caveat disclosure check, packaging, versioning — and attaches the finished deliverable to the originating Task. Nothing leaves the lab without surviving both the science gate upstream and the paper-trail gate here.
