The Research team owns everything between an incoming question and an approved evidence package. The Chief Scientist decomposes each engagement into survey and analysis workstreams; the Literature Reviewer maps what is already known, the Data Analyst measures what is not, and the Methodology Critic attacks both deliverables before anything is allowed to count as a finding. The team's output is never a document for readers — it is a critiqued, traceable evidence base that the Publications team can turn into one, with every open caveat logged rather than smoothed over.
