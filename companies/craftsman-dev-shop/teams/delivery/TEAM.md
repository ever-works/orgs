Delivery is the whole engineering line of Craftsman Dev Shop in one team: the Lead Engineer plans and builds test-first, the Code Reviewer holds the merge bar, the QA Analyst attacks the running change, and the Release Engineer ships it and proves it landed. The team's charter is simple — no slice skips a station, every handoff is an artifact, and the person who finds a problem routes it to the person who owns the fix rather than patching around it. Delivery measures itself on defects caught before release, not on speed through the stations.
