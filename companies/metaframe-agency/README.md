# Metaframe Agency

A prebuilt AI creative-strategy and meta-skills agency for Ever Works. Bring it a problem that resists a direct answer — a decision with no obvious winner, a question nobody has researched properly, an AI workflow that leaks time — and the org classifies it into a lane (thinking, research, or workflow), applies the right named method, and returns the analysis with the method visible.

## Org structure

**Principal — Iris Calloway** (root): classifies every ask into a lane, decomposes it into Tasks, and gates all client-bound artifacts on one test — can the client act without a follow-up question.

### Thinking team (manager: Frameworks Lead)
- **Tomas Ferreira — Frameworks Lead**: selects and runs the mental model (first principles, inversion, second-order, root cause, leverage, subtraction) as a written pass with the selection rationale shown.
- **Jonas Lindqvist — Workflow Optimizer**: maps AI-assisted workflows as they actually run and prescribes ranked removals, reorderings, and checkpoints.

### Research team (manager: Research Methodologist)
- **Amara Diallo — Research Methodologist**: opens every study with a written plan and evidence bar, executes into rated findings, grades every memo before it ships.
- **Saoirse Byrne — Insight Writer**: synthesizes rated findings into answer-first memos with claim-level citations and an honest disconfirming section.

**Elena Vargas — Facilitator** (reports to Principal): designs and runs structured working sessions for engagements where the client's own team must do the thinking, and returns decision records.

## Skills (12)

Thinking frameworks: `first-principles-pass`, `assumption-audit`, `inversion-check`, `second-order-scan`, `root-cause-drill`, `leverage-audit`, `subtraction-pass`.
Research methodology: `research-plan`, `options-comparison`, `synthesis-memo`.
Process and continuity: `workflow-review`, `handoff-note`.

## Curation note

Curated from an upstream org of 6 agents and 35 skills: the upstream's twelve thinking-framework commands and eight research templates were distilled into ten original playbooks (several frameworks merged where they share a discipline, e.g. leverage analysis absorbs the 80/20 and highest-leverage-action models), and its tooling/automation wing was generalized into a platform-neutral `workflow-review` practice. Vendor-specific skill-authoring, hook, and app-development expertise was left out as out of scope for a strategy agency.

## After import

Create a Work for your engagement and assign the opening problem as a Task to the Principal. Work flows Principal → lane owner (Frameworks Lead, Research Methodologist, or Facilitator) → specialists → back through the review gates to you. Expect artifacts on the originating Tasks: an engagement brief, framework passes, research plans and synthesis memos, workflow reviews, and decision records.
