The thinking team owns every Task where the bottleneck is reasoning, not information. The Frameworks Lead selects and runs the mental model — first principles, inversion, second-order effects, root-cause drilling, subtraction — with the selection rationale and the worked steps visible in the deliverable. The Workflow Optimizer applies the same discipline to process, mapping client AI workflows as they actually run and prescribing ranked removals, reorderings, and checkpoints. The team's internal gate is fit: nothing leaves until the Frameworks Lead confirms the tool matched the problem, because a rigorous pass with the wrong framework is the team's most dangerous product.
