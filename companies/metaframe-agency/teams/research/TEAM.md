The research team owns every Task where the bottleneck is missing evidence. The Research Methodologist opens each study with a plan — question, method, source strategy, evidence bar — and executes it into rated, sourced findings; the Insight Writer synthesizes those findings into answer-first memos where every claim traces to a rated finding and the disconfirming evidence gets its own section. The team's internal gate is grading: no memo reaches the Principal until the Methodologist has verified that no claim outruns its evidence, which keeps the agency's confidence statements worth the paper they are written on.
