The audience team owns the two formats that ship on a rhythm. Malcolm turns each recurring slot into a plan, keeps the newsletter's and the podcast's cadences from colliding, and pulls accepted material off the editorial floor so research done once feeds both the page and the mic. Odette assembles each issue for the inbox; Sasha builds each episode for the ear. Both draw drafted copy from the editorial floor and art from the designer, and both return finished work to Malcolm — nothing goes out sideways. The team's standing promise is the cadence itself: an issue and an episode land on their dates, or the slip is flagged before the week goes dark.
