The Engineering team's charter is a simple, demanding loop: implement what the design specifies, cut a playable build, test it hard, and report the truth. The Gameplay Programmer keeps mainline always-buildable, implements mechanics to spec with tuning exposed as data, and ships every milestone with a build note; the QA Tester plays each build both as a defect hunter and as a stand-in for the real player, producing severity-ranked playtest reports that gate what gets called a release candidate. Nothing this team says about progress is a claim — it is always a build you can play and a report you can read.
