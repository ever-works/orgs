# Beacon Agency

Beacon Agency is a prebuilt AI company for the Ever Works "orgs" catalog. It
models a full-service client-services agency that runs a portfolio of accounts
end to end — winning the work, scoping and pricing it, producing the creative,
buying the media, and reporting the results — with a single owner accountable for
each account from first pitch through renewal.

Import it when you want an org that behaves like an agency: new business enters at
the top, becomes a scoped and signed statement of work, is planned and produced by
specialist teams, and is reviewed continuously against what the client was
promised.

### Teams

- **Accounts** — manager Account Director, with the Account Manager. Owns the
  client relationship and the commercials: proposals, statements of work, the
  scope gate, and account health.
- **Delivery** — manager Delivery Lead, with the Project Coordinator. Turns
  accepted scope into a resourced schedule and keeps it true day to day.
- **Creative** — manager Creative Lead, with the Copywriter and Designer. Makes
  everything the client sees, from a shared idea, behind one critique.

### Skills

Five reusable playbooks ship with the company and are attached to the agents who
run them:

- **account-health-review** — score an account green / yellow / red and act early.
- **client-proposal-rfp** — qualify, shape, price, and win new business.
- **campaign-brief** — turn a client ask into a briefed, buildable campaign.
- **quarterly-business-review** — report results and set what the next quarter buys.
- **scope-and-sow** — write the scope that separates paid work from free work.

## What to expect after import

- Nine agents arrive wired into the reporting line above, with their skills
  attached and their roles, handoffs, and principles written into each AGENTS.md.
- Three teams arrive with their managers and members resolved.
- The five skills become available to the agents that reference them.
- Agents run on Ever Works: they receive Tasks, work inside the account's Work,
  use their attached skills, and coordinate through Tasks and explicit handoffs.

Point the company at a real client account by creating a Work for it and opening
the first Tasks — a qualified opportunity to the Account Director, or an approved
brief to the Creative Lead — and the reporting line does the rest.
