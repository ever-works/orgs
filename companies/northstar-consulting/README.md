# Northstar Product Consulting

A prebuilt AI product-strategy consultancy for Ever Works. Hand it a fuzzy product question — "should we build this?", "what do we charge?", "why aren't users upgrading?" — and the org runs discovery, synthesizes a strategy, and returns a sequenced roadmap with pricing recommendations.

## Org structure

**Managing Partner — Margot Hale** (root): frames every ask as a decision, decomposes it into Tasks, and gates all client-facing deliverables.

### Discovery team (manager: Product Strategist)
- **Priya Sen — Principal Product Strategist**: turns decisions into research questions, synthesizes evidence into ranked strategy briefs.
- **Rafael Ortiz — UX Researcher**: interviews and behavioral evidence, delivered as patterned findings with counts.
- **Ingrid Voss — Market Analyst**: competitor landscapes, sizing chains, positioning gaps, competitor pricing tables.

### Delivery team (manager: Roadmap Planner)
- **Dana Whitfield — Roadmap Planner**: slices strategy into shippable candidates, stack-ranks, sequences into a three-horizon roadmap.
- **Theo Marsh — Pricing Analyst**: value-metric-anchored pricing reviews with modeled alternatives and migration risks.

## Skills

- `discovery-interview-guide` — planning, running, and coding user discovery interviews.
- `prioritization-framework` — evidence-discounted scoring with a forced stack-rank.
- `pricing-review` — packaging audit, competitor comparison, alternative modeling.

## After import

Create a Work for your engagement and assign the opening question as a Task to the Managing Partner. Work flows Managing Partner → Discovery (evidence and strategy brief) → Delivery (roadmap and pricing) → back through the partner gate to you. Expect artifacts on the originating Tasks: an engagement brief, evidence packets, a ranked strategy brief, a sequenced roadmap, and pricing recommendations.
