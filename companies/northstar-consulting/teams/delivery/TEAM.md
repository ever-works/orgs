Delivery owns the back half of the engagement: everything after the strategy brief clears the partner gate. The Roadmap Planner slices strategic bets into shippable candidates, ranks them, and sequences them into a three-horizon roadmap against the client's real capacity; the Pricing Analyst takes the monetization-sensitive items and returns value-metric-anchored pricing recommendations with the migration risks named. The team's discipline is traceability — every roadmap position and every pricing verdict must cite the discovery evidence it rests on, so the Managing Partner's coherence review is a check, not an archaeology dig.
