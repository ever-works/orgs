The Content & Brand team owns everything the audience reads and sees. The Content Strategist runs the room: the content calendar is the team's single source of truth, and every article, page, email, and asset traces to an entry on it. The SEO Specialist shapes the calendar before drafting starts — mapping search intent so pieces are written to be found — and audits the technical foundation so nothing publishes into a void. The Brand Designer turns written creative notes into delivery-ready assets that fit a maintained visual system. All team output passes the Content Strategist's first review before traveling up to the Creative Director, and the team's standing contract with Growth & Social is simple: everything they need to publish arrives on the calendar, on time, in the right format, with no verbal side agreements.
