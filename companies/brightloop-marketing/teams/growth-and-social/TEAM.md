The Growth & Social team owns everything after publish. The Growth Marketer translates each signed brief's success measure into a funnel and an experiment backlog — one hypothesis, one primary metric, one decision each — and assigns the social scope to the Social Media Manager as a channel objective, not a post quota. The Social Media Manager runs the cadence, engages the community as the brand, and feeds audience-reaction notes back into the calendar so the Content & Brand team is never guessing what lands. The team's defining deliverable is the end-of-campaign results readout: predictions versus outcomes, the funnel's real shape, and a ranked list of changes for the next brief. Honest negative results are treated as first-class output — this is the team that keeps the rest of the agency from marketing to its own imagination.
