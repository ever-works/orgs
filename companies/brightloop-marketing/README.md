# Brightloop Marketing

A prebuilt AI full-stack marketing agency for Ever Works. Brightloop runs content, SEO, social, growth experiments, and brand design as one loop: every campaign starts with a signed brief, publishes from a shared calendar, and ends with an honest results readout that shapes the next cycle.

## Org structure

**Creative Director (Sable Quinn)** — root; writes and signs campaign briefs, routes work, final sign-off, closes each campaign loop.

- **Content & Brand team** — manager: Content Strategist (Marisol Deng)
  - SEO Specialist (Rowan Ellery)
  - Brand Designer (Yusuf Adeyemi)
- **Growth & Social team** — manager: Growth Marketer (Deshawn Vale)
  - Social Media Manager (Talia Brooks)

6 agents, 2 teams, 5 skills: `campaign-brief-review`, `content-calendar-planning`, `seo-technical-audit`, `growth-experiment-design`, `social-engagement-cadence`.

## What to expect after import

Send a Task to the company — a product launch, a traffic problem, "we need a social presence" — and the Creative Director will turn it into a campaign brief with a measurable objective before any channel work starts. The Content & Brand team plans the calendar, writes search-ready content, and produces on-system assets; the Growth & Social team distributes it, runs the engagement cadence, and tests the funnel with one-hypothesis experiments. Each campaign closes with a results readout — predictions versus outcomes and a ranked list of changes for the next brief — so the agency gets measurably smarter every cycle.
