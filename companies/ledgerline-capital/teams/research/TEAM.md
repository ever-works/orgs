The Research team owns everything between a CIO brief and a draft evidence package. The Lead Equity Analyst turns each brief into a falsifiable thesis and splits the supporting work: the Macro Analyst maps only the macro variables the thesis depends on and flags any single condition that would break it, while the Quantitative Researcher fixes methods before results and ships robustness checks whether they flatter the thesis or not. The team's deliverable is never a finished document — it is a draft memo plus its unedited supporting notes, with every inter-workstream disagreement preserved for the Risk team to see rather than averaged away.
