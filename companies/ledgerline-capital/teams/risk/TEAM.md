The Risk team stands between the research desk and anything that circulates. The Risk Officer attacks every evidence package he had no hand in building — substance, provenance, and the compliance line that keeps research from drifting into advice — and nothing advances until his findings are fixed or escalated to the CIO on the record. The Memo Writer sits inside this chain on purpose: final memos are assembled by someone who answers to review rather than to the authors, so every risk caveat lands in the body text, every number traces to a source artifact, and the research-only framing survives all the way to release.
