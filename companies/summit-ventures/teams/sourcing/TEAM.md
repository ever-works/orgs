The Sourcing team owns everything between a thesis and a shortlist. The Sourcing Lead turns the General Partner's mandate into explicit search criteria and disqualifiers, then splits the field into batches the Investment Analyst screens company by company. The Analyst gathers the few facts that decide a first look, scores each candidate against the rubric, and sketches a quick market landscape when a category is unfamiliar — always labelling what is known versus inferred. The team's deliverable is never a decision; it is a ranked shortlist where every company carries a one-pager and a defensible reason to pursue, and every passed company carries a logged reason so the same ground is not re-walked.
