The Portfolio team owns every company after it is invested. The Portfolio Lead runs a standing health-review cadence — pulling KPIs, rating each company's direction with a reason, and triaging the firm's attention onto the companies where it changes an outcome. The Founder Liaison runs the check-ins that feed it, gathering each company's state in its own words, turning founder asks into tracked internal Tasks, and surfacing the early, awkward signals before they become surprises. The team's deliverable is a portfolio health review with a ranked attention list and support plans tied to specific companies — stewardship and analysis only, never advice to buy, sell, or move money — with anything material escalated to the General Partner.
