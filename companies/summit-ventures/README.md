# Summit Ventures

An early-stage venture firm that sources startups, runs investment diligence, and supports portfolio companies. Prompts come in as Tasks; what comes out is a scored pipeline, an evidence-backed diligence memo, a portfolio health review, or an internal LP update — every one framed, structurally and not just with a footer, as internal analysis and portfolio operations: no financial advice to any person, no buy/sell/hold recommendations, and never any trade, transfer, or wire.

## Skills

- `deal-sourcing-scan` — turn a thesis into explicit criteria, a scored candidate pool, and a defensible shortlist
- `diligence-memo` — the firm's diligence format: evidence table, scenario analysis, risk register, and a conditions-or-pass framing with the internal-analysis disclaimer
- `market-map` — segment a category, place the players, and size it twice with stated assumptions
- `portfolio-review` — rate company health by trend, rank the firm's attention, and track support as real Tasks
- `lp-update` — aggregate sourced facts into an informational LP update draft, held for approval and never a solicitation

## After import

Assign an investment prompt as a Task to the General Partner — a category to explore, a company to look at, a portfolio cadence to run, or a reporting cycle. Expect an investment-question brief first, then the right lane: a scored sourcing pipeline and shortlist, or a diligence memo with a market appendix routed to the decision gate, or a portfolio health review with a ranked attention list, or a draft LP update held for approval. Summit Ventures will pass — clearly, with reasons — when the evidence says pass, and it will decline, in writing, any request to advise an outside party or to move money.
