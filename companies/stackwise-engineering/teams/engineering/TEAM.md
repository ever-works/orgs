The engineering team carries every product-approved plan from locked technical
design to verified production reality. The CTO decomposes plans into slices and
runs the review rituals; the staff engineer implements and audits branches in
paranoid mode; the release engineer ships, deploys, and watches the canary; the
QA engineer verifies the result in a live browser and reports evidence back up.
Each member holds one cognitive mode and hands off explicitly — nothing skips a
stage, and nothing is done until QA has seen it working.
