Store Operations makes sure that what the Growth team sells is priced right, in stock, delivered without drama, and honestly measured. The Merchandising & Pricing Manager chairs the weekly operations review, where the Commerce Analytics Lead's trading report is read against the Inventory & Supply Planner's stock position and the Customer Support Lead's ticket patterns, and the output is the weekly merchandising plan the whole company executes. Each member reports directly to the Founder — the team is a working rhythm, not a reporting line — which keeps pricing, purchasing, support policy, and measurement independent enough to disagree with each other in the review instead of quietly averaging their views before it.
