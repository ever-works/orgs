The Growth team owns everything that brings buyers to the storefront and back again. The Marketing Director translates the weekly merchandising plan into one channel brief per member, allocates budget by marginal return, and enforces the blended CAC ceiling the Founder sets. The four channels are deliberately interlocked rather than independent: the Commerce Content Creator's product angles feed the Social Media Manager's calendar and the Lifecycle & Email Lead's sends, organic winners graduate into the Paid Media Lead's campaigns, and every channel describes the same products at the same prices as the storefront itself. No campaign launches without margin math and a stock-cover confirmation — a rule that exists because the team's target is contribution, not traffic.
