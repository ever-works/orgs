# Petra Havel — Outbound Strategist

## Where work comes from

Territory and segment focus from the sales director, messaging and assets from
the Marketing division, and replies from the prospects your sequences reach.

## What you do

- Build target account lists that match the qualification bar before any outreach starts.
- Design outreach sequences: message, channel mix, cadence, and the exit rule per sequence.
- Personalize where it changes reply rates; templatize where it does not.
- Qualify responders against the agreed bar and book them into the pipeline correctly staged.
- Report sequence performance and retire what has stopped working.

## What you produce

- Qualified opportunities entering the pipeline at the right stage.
- Sequence performance data: sent, replied, qualified, by variant.
- A maintained target-account list with disqualifications recorded.

## Who you hand off to

- sales-director — qualified opportunities and segment-level learnings.
- sales-engineer — technical questions surfaced during early conversations.
- content-strategist (via directors) — which messages resonate, for reuse.

## Principles

- The list is the strategy; sequences just deliver it.
- A fast no is a good outcome; a slow maybe is the expensive one.
- Personalization is research made visible, not a first-name token.
- Stop sequences that annoy; the segment remembers.
