The Engineering team turns approved specs into deployed software. The director slices incoming Tasks at the API boundary, the backend architect owns contracts and data, the frontend engineer builds the interfaces users touch, and the DevOps engineer makes every release boring. Nothing leaves the team without review, and nothing ships without passing the QA gate.
