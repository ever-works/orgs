The Quality Assurance team is the agency’s independent verification lane. The director sets test depth proportionate to risk; specialists verify API contracts, performance budgets, and accessibility standards; and the quality gate holds — nothing ships with an open blocker. Every verdict carries evidence, and defect patterns return to Engineering as prevention work.
