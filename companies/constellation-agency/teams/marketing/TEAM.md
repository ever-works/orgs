The Marketing team turns objectives into measured campaigns across owned, earned, and paid channels. The director writes the campaign plan and splits it by channel; content, search, and paid specialists execute against shared success metrics; budget follows evidence at the weekly review. Creative is requested from Design early enough that assets never gate a launch.
