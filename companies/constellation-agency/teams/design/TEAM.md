The Design team owns how everything the agency makes looks, flows, and speaks visually. Structure is settled before surface: the UX architect fixes flows, the UI designer finishes screens against the design system, and the brand designer keeps identity coherent from product to campaign to proposal. Every deliverable passes one structured critique before it leaves the division.
