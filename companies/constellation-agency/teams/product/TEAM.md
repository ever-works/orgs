The Product team decides what gets built and shepherds it to launched. Evidence from the insights analyst feeds prioritization, the product manager turns initiatives into actionable specs, and the delivery producer runs the cross-division schedule and launch checklists. The director owns the roadmap, the non-goals, and the go/no-go recommendation.
