The Game Development team builds interactive experiences on a playable-milestone rhythm: prototype, vertical slice, content-complete, polish. Design specs carry numbers, tuning is data-driven, the art pipeline enforces budgets at import, and the fun bar is a gate a milestone can fail. Scope is cut at the milestone, never at the deadline.
